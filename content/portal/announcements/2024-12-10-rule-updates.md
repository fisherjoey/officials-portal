## Important Rule Changes

Your League has announced several rule changes for the 2024-2025 season that all officials must be aware of:

### 1. Timing Modifications
- Junior level games will now use modified timing procedures
- Elementary level games remain with standard timing rules

### 2. Penalty Limit Changes
- Senior level games now allow modified penalty limits
- All other levels remain at standard penalty limits

### 3. Technical Violation Procedures
- New procedures for handling technical violations have been implemented
- Please review the full documentation in the Resources section

### Training Sessions

Mandatory training sessions on these rule changes will be held:
- **January 15, 2025** - 6:00 PM at Central Community Center
- **January 17, 2025** - 6:00 PM at West Recreation Complex

All officials must attend one session to maintain their certification.
