We're excited to announce that registration is now open for our comprehensive New Officials Training Program! This program is designed for individuals who want to start their journey as a certified official.

## Program Overview

Our New Officials Training Program provides everything you need to begin your officiating career:

- **Online Learning Modules**: Complete foundational coursework at your own pace
- **In-Person Workshops**: Hands-on training sessions with experienced instructors
- **On-Field Sessions**: Practice officiating with mentor support
- **Certification Exam**: Complete the certification process

## Who Should Apply?

This program is perfect for:

- Individuals new to officiating
- Former players looking to stay involved in the sport
- Parents wanting to contribute to youth athletics
- Anyone interested in a flexible part-time opportunity

## Key Dates

- **Registration Opens**: January 15
- **Registration Closes**: February 15
- **Online Modules**: February 20 - March 15
- **In-Person Workshops**: March 22-23
- **On-Field Sessions**: April (dates TBD)

## Benefits of Becoming an Official

- Flexible schedule - work as many or as few games as you want
- Competitive compensation
- Stay connected to the sport you love
- Make a positive impact on young athletes
- Join a supportive community of fellow officials

## How to Register

Visit our [Become an Official](/become-a-referee) page to complete your application. Space is limited, so register early to secure your spot.

Questions? Contact our Education Committee at education@example.com or reach out through our [contact form](/contact).
