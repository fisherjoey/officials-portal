## Overview

This comprehensive one-day clinic is designed for individuals new to officiating. No prior experience is required. You'll learn the fundamentals of officiating, including rules, mechanics, positioning, and game management.

## What You'll Learn

- Basic rules and their application
- Positioning and movement
- Signaling mechanics
- Game management fundamentals
- Communication with players and coaches
- Pre-game and post-game procedures

## What to Bring

- Comfortable athletic clothing
- Appropriate footwear for your sport
- Water bottle
- Notebook and pen
- Whistle (if you have one; we'll provide if needed)

## Schedule

- 9:00 AM - Registration and welcome
- 9:30 AM - Rules overview
- 11:00 AM - Mechanics and positioning
- 12:00 PM - Lunch break
- 1:00 PM - On-field practice
- 3:00 PM - Game simulation
- 4:00 PM - Wrap-up and next steps
